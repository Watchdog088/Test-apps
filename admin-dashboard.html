📋 Marketplace Feature Breakdown 17 Core Features
1️⃣ Product Listings
Create, edit, delete listings. Multi-image upload (Cloudinary CDN). Title, description, price, category, condition, stock. Search-indexed.
2️⃣ Shopping Cart
Persistent cart (localStorage + Firestore sync). Add/remove/update quantity. Multi-seller checkout. Variation support (size, color).
3️⃣ Order Management
Full order lifecycle: pending → confirmed → shipped → delivered. Order history. Cancel orders. Real-time status updates via Firestore.
4️⃣ Payment Processing
Stripe/PayPal/Apple Pay/Google Pay. Saved payment methods. Auto fee calculation (5% platform + 2.9% Stripe). Secure tokenized transactions.
5️⃣ Seller Management
Seller profiles, store banners, bio, ratings. Sales dashboard. Earnings tracker. Seller verification badges. Payout to bank/PayPal.
6️⃣ Product Reviews
Star ratings (1-5), photo reviews, verified purchase badge. Helpful votes. Seller responses. Avg rating visible on listing.
7️⃣ Shipping & Tracking
Multi-carrier shipping (USPS, UPS, FedEx). Real-time tracking numbers. Estimated delivery. Free shipping threshold. Shipping label generation.
8️⃣ Disputes & Resolution
Buyer opens dispute. Admin mediates. Evidence upload (photos, messages). 15-day auto-resolve. Refund or seller release.
9️⃣ Returns & Refunds
30-day return policy. Reason selection. Return shipping label. Admin approval workflow. Stripe refund processing. Partial/full refunds.
🔟 Inventory Management
Stock level tracking. Low-stock alerts (< 5 units). Out-of-stock auto-hide. Bulk inventory updates. SKU management.
1️⃣1️⃣ Wishlist
Save products for later. Wishlist sharing. Price drop alerts. Move to cart. Social wishlist visibility.
1️⃣2️⃣ Buyer Protection
Money-back guarantee. 30-day protection window. Fraud detection. Unauthorized charge protection. Escrow-style payment hold until delivery.
1️⃣3️⃣ Seller Protection
Protects verified sellers from false claims. Evidence review system. Chargeback protection. Seller score maintained.
1️⃣4️⃣ Product Search & Discovery
Full-text search with filters. Category browsing. Price range slider. Sort by: price, rating, newest, popular. Sponsored listings.
1️⃣5️⃣ Seller Analytics
Sales charts (daily/weekly/monthly). Revenue breakdown. Top products. Customer demographics. Conversion rate. Return rate per product.
1️⃣6️⃣ Marketplace Notifications
Order confirmations, shipping alerts, dispute updates, review received, low stock warnings, payment received — via OneSignal push.
1️⃣7️⃣ Multi-Payment Methods
Credit/debit cards (Stripe), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, LynkCoins (in-app currency), Buy Now Pay Later (Affirm), Gift cards.
