## 2. BEFORE YOU FILE — DO THESE FREE SEARCHES FIRST

**You must do this or your application will be rejected.**

### Step A: Search the USPTO TESS Database
**URL:** https://tmsearch.uspto.gov/search/search-information

1. Go to that URL
2. Click **"Basic Word Mark Search (New User)"**
3. In the search box, type: `LYNKAPP` → Click Search
4. Repeat with: `LYNK APP`
5. Repeat with: `LYNK`
6. Look at every result — if another company already has "LYNKAPP" in Class 42, you need an attorney before filing
7. If results show no exact match for your classes → you're clear to file ✅

### Step B: Search Google + App Stores
1. Google: `"LynkApp" trademark`
2. Google Play Store: search "LynkApp"
3. Apple App Store: search "LynkApp"
4. If there's another app using this exact name → consult an attorney

### Step C: Search the Copyright Office (Optional but Smart)
- **URL:** https://www.copyright.gov/search
- Search "LynkApp" — if you see a registration, it means someone else filed your software copyright (not likely, but worth checking)

#### SCREEN 4: Filing Basis

This is where you choose HOW you're claiming rights. You have two options:

**If the app is LIVE (launched) — Select this:**
- ✅ **"Use in Commerce" (Section 1(a))**

**If the app is NOT live yet — Select this:**

> **My Recommendation:** If LynkApp is currently live at lynkapp.com, file under Section 1(a) "Use in Commerce". This is stronger and costs the same.

## 5. INTERNATIONAL PROTECTION (Optional — Post-Launch)

Once you have a US trademark registration, you can extend it globally through **Madrid Protocol** via the USPTO:

| Country/Region | System | Cost (approx.) |
|----------------|--------|----------------|
| European Union (all 27 countries) | EUIPO | ~€850 |
| United Kingdom | UK IPO | ~£200 |
| Canada | CIPO | ~$400 CAD |
| Australia | IP Australia | ~$250 AUD |
| International (60+ countries) | WIPO Madrid | ~$850 USD base + per country |

**To file internationally:**
1. First get your US registration (takes 8-14 months)
2. Then go to: https://www.wipo.int/madrid/en/
3. Or use the USPTO's international filing portal: https://tmog.uspto.gov

> **Recommendation for LynkApp:** File US first. After registration is granted (~12 months), file EU + UK + Canada bundle.

## 6. WHAT TO PUT IN THE APP FOR TRADEMARK NOTICES

Once your application is filed, you can start using trademark notices in the app immediately. Here's exactly where and what to put:

### A. In the App Footer (All Pages)

Add this to the bottom of every page of your app:

```
© 2024-2026 LynkApp LLC. All rights reserved.
LYNKAPP™ is a trademark of LynkApp LLC, pending registration with the USPTO.
```

> **Note:** Use ™ (not ®) until your registration is officially GRANTED by the USPTO. Only use ® after you receive your registration certificate.

### B. In the Landing Page Footer

In your `LandingPage.jsx`, the footer section should read:

```
LynkApp™ | © 2026 LynkApp LLC
LYNKAPP, LYNK, and the LynkApp logo are trademarks of LynkApp LLC.
All rights reserved. Patent pending.
```

### C. In the App's "About" Page

In `AboutPage.jsx`, add a section:

```
Trademarks

LynkApp™ and the LynkApp logo are trademarks and/or registered trademarks 
of LynkApp LLC in the United States and other countries. All other trademarks 
are the property of their respective owners.
```

### D. In the Terms of Service Page

Add to your `TermsPage.jsx`:

```
Intellectual Property

"LynkApp," "LYNK," the LynkApp logo, and all related names, logos, product 
and service names, designs, and slogans are trademarks of LynkApp LLC or its 
affiliates or licensors. You must not use such marks without the prior written 
permission of LynkApp LLC. All other names, logos, product and service names, 
designs, and slogans on this app are the trademarks of their respective owners.
```

### E. In the App's Splash Screen / Login Screen

Somewhere on the login or splash screen (can be small text at the bottom):

```
LynkApp™  |  © 2026 LynkApp LLC
```

### F. Symbol Usage Rules

| Symbol | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| ™ | Use immediately after filing your trademark application with the USPTO |
| ® | Use ONLY after USPTO issues the registration certificate (8–14 months later) |
| © | Use for copyright — applies to your code, designs, text immediately |

## 7. COPYRIGHT YOUR SOFTWARE (DO THIS TODAY — FREE)

In addition to trademark, register your software code with the US Copyright Office. This is **separate from trademark** and costs only **$65 online**.

**URL:** https://www.copyright.gov/registration/

1. Click "Register a New Claim"
2. Log in or create an account
3. Select: **"Computer File" or "Other Digital Content"**
4. Fill in:
   - Title: `LynkApp`
   - Year of Creation: `2024` (or when you started)
   - Author/Claimant: Your name or LLC name
   - Nature of authorship: "Computer program"
5. Upload a representative portion of your source code (50–100 pages as PDF — does NOT have to be the full codebase)
6. Pay $65
7. You'll receive a certificate in 3–6 months

> ✅ Copyright registration lets you sue for statutory damages ($750–$30,000 per violation) if someone steals your code.

## 8. WHAT TO DO AFTER FILING

### Immediately After Submission:
1. You'll receive a **Serial Number** (e.g., 98/123,456) — **save this number**
2. Email confirmation will be sent to your registered email
3. Start using ™ symbol in the app immediately

### 3–6 Months After Filing:
1. Check status at: **https://tsdr.uspto.gov**
2. Enter your serial number
3. If status says **"Office Action Issued"** — you have 3 months to respond. Read it carefully — usually about clarifying the description or proving no conflict with another mark

### 8–14 Months — Publication for Opposition:
1. Your mark will be published in the **Official Gazette**
2. Third parties have 30 days to oppose
3. If no opposition → registration is approved

### After Registration:
1. You receive your **Registration Certificate** — switch from ™ to ®
2. File a **Section 8 Declaration** between years 5–6 (prove you're still using the mark) — $225 fee
3. File for **10-year renewal** at year 9–10 — $300 fee
