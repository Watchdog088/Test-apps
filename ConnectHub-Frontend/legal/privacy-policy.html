Lynk Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

Last Updated: August 16, 2025
Effective Date: August 16, 2025
Quick Summary: We collect the information you provide to create your profile and deliver our social media and dating services. We use this data to help you connect with others, but we never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Data Type Examples Purpose
Profile Information Name, age, photos, bio, interests Create your profile and show to potential matches
Contact Information Email address, phone number Account verification and important notifications
Content Posts, messages, comments, photos Social media and dating interactions
Preferences Dating preferences, privacy settings Personalize your experience and matching

1.2 Information We Automatically Collect

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Location Data: Approximate location for dating matches (when permitted)
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Services

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Display your profile to other users
  • Facilitate social media interactions (posts, likes, comments)
  • Enable dating features and matching algorithms
  • Process and deliver messages

2.2 Personalization & Matching

  • Show you relevant content and potential matches
  • Improve our matching algorithms using AI/ML
  • Customize your feed based on interests and interactions
  • Suggest friends and connections

2.3 Safety & Security

  • Verify identity and prevent fraud
  • Detect and prevent spam and abuse
  • Moderate content for safety
  • Investigate violations of our Terms of Service

3. Information Sharing

3.1 With Other Users

Your profile information is visible to other users based on your privacy settings. This includes:

  • Profile photos and basic information
  • Posts you make publicly
  • Your activity in the dating section (when using dating features)

3.2 With Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform:

  • Cloud hosting services (AWS, Google Cloud, etc.)
  • Email and communication services
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Payment processing (for premium features)

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal process
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Ensure user safety
  • Prevent fraud or abuse

4. Location Information

Dating Feature: Location data helps us show you potential matches nearby. You can control location sharing in your settings.

4.1 How We Use Location

  • Show distance to potential matches
  • Enable location-based features
  • Improve matching algorithms
  • Prevent spam and abuse

4.2 Location Controls

  • Turn location sharing on/off anytime
  • Choose location accuracy level
  • Opt out of location-based matching

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and testing
  • Multi-factor authentication options
  • Secure data centers with access controls
  • Employee training on data protection

5.2 Your Security

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Keep your app updated
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Download a copy of your data
  • Control who can see your profile

6.2 Privacy Settings

You can control your privacy through settings:

  • Profile visibility settings
  • Location sharing preferences
  • Communication preferences
  • Data sharing controls

7. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account:

  • Profile data is deleted within 30 days
  • Messages may be retained for safety reasons
  • Some data may be kept for legal compliance
  • Analytics data is anonymized

8. Age Requirements

Lynk is only available to users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover that a minor has created an account, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Users

Lynk operates globally. Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

9.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

EU users have additional rights including:

  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to be forgotten
  • Right to withdraw consent

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze app performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification
  • In-app notification
  • Website banner
