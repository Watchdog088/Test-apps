Lynk Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

Last Updated: August 17, 2025
Effective Date: August 17, 2025
Version: 2.0 - Production Release
Quick Summary: We collect the information you provide to create your profile and deliver our comprehensive social media and dating services. We use this data to help you connect with others through AI-powered matching, video processing, and enhanced features, but we never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Data Type Examples Purpose
Profile Information Name, age, photos, bio, interests Create your profile and show to potential matches
Contact Information Email address, phone number Account verification and important notifications
Content Posts, messages, comments, photos, videos, music Social media and dating interactions, video processing
Media Files Photos, videos, audio recordings, music uploads Content sharing, watermarking, music synchronization
Communication Data Voice calls, video calls, chat messages Real-time communication and interaction features
Preferences Dating preferences, privacy settings Personalize your experience and matching

1.2 Information We Automatically Collect

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our app, features used, time spent, engagement patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, camera/microphone capabilities
  • Location Data: Approximate location for dating matches (when permitted)
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, error logs, performance metrics
  • AI Analysis Data: Content analysis results, matching algorithm inputs, behavioral patterns
  • Media Metadata: Video quality settings, processing preferences, upload timestamps
  • Gamification Data: Points, achievements, progress tracking, reward history

  • Apply automatic watermarking with Lynk branding

  • Generate thumbnails and preview content

2.4 Safety & Security

  • Verify identity and prevent fraud using advanced algorithms
  • Detect and prevent spam and abuse through AI monitoring
  • Moderate content for safety using automated and manual review
  • Investigate violations of our Terms of Service

3. Information Sharing

3.1 With Other Users

Your profile information is visible to other users based on your privacy settings. This includes:

  • Profile photos and basic information
  • Posts you make publicly
  • Your activity in the dating section (when using dating features)

3.2 With Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform:

  • Cloud hosting services (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure) for data storage and processing
  • Email and communication services for notifications and support
  • Analytics and performance monitoring services
  • Payment processing services (Stripe, PayPal) for premium features
  • Video processing services (FFmpeg) for media optimization
  • AI/ML services for content analysis and matching algorithms
  • Content delivery networks (CDNs) for global media distribution
  • Security services for fraud detection and prevention

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal process
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Ensure user safety
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
